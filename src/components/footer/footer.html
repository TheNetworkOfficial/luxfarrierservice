About Lux Farrier Service

Lux Farrier Service is owned and operated by passionate horsemen & horsewomen committed to the health and success of your animals. With over a decade of combined experience working in the equine medical and podiatry field, we serve the farrier needs of all horses across the greater Montana area, from barefoot trims to laminitus rehabilitation.
