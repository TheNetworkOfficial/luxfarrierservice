Welcome to Lux Farrier Services

At Lux Farrier Services, we are passionate horsemen and horsewomen committed to the health and success of your animals. With over a decade of combined experience in equine medical and podiatry fields, we proudly serve the farrier needs of horses across Montana, from Bozeman to Missoula, and everywhere in between. From barefoot trims to laminitis rehabilitation, your horse's care is our priority.
